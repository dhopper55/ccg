Cort Guitar Serial Number Decoder

Cort Guitars (a subsidiary of Cor-Tek Corporation), founded in 1973 by Park Yung-ho, is a major South Korean musical instrument manufacturer based in Seoul. Known for high-quality production at accessible prices, Cort produces over a million guitars annually, including acoustic, electric, and bass models. The company manufactures for major global brands—such as Ibanez, Schecter, and PRS—while maintaining its own brand line.
