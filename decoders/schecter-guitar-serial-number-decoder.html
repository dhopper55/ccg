Schecter Guitar Serial Number Decoder

Schecter Guitar Research is a prominent American manufacturer founded in 1976 by David Schecter, initially specializing in high-quality replacement parts for Fender and Gibson. Known for electric guitars, basses, and acoustic instruments, the brand has evolved from a custom shop to a globally recognized manufacturer, frequently utilized by artists like Avenged Sevenfold and The Cure.
