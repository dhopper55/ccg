How to decode a Godin serial number

A quick manual method when you don't want to use a decoder.

Godin guitar serial numbers generally follow a system where the first two digits indicate the year of manufacture (e.g., “04” indicates 2004). For 7‑digit numbers, often seen in the 90s, the first digit represents the year. For newer, 12‑digit codes (approx. 2007/2008 onwards), the first two digits still commonly indicate the year, while the seventh digit indicates if it is a factory second.
